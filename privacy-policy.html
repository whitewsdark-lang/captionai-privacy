Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CaptionAI ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application CaptionAI (the "App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CaptionAI is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not store the content you type into the app
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs

2. How the App Works

When you use CaptionAI to generate captions, the text you enter is sent directly to our AI processing server solely for the purpose of generating caption suggestions. This content is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers after your request is complete.

3. Third-Party AI Service

CaptionAI uses Groq's AI API to generate captions. Your input text is transmitted to Groq's servers for processing. Please refer to Groq's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

4. Device Permissions

CaptionAI may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access: Required to send requests to our AI service and receive generated captions.
  • Clipboard access: Used only when you tap the "Copy" button to copy a caption to your clipboard.

5. Children's Privacy

CaptionAI is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: captionai.app@gmail.com
